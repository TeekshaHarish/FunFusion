How to Play Tic Tac Toe

1. Board Setup: TicTacToe is played on a grid of 3x3 squares. Each player takes turns marking a square with their respective symbol.
2. Players: There are two players in this game, Player X and Player O. Player X goes first and marks their squares with an "X" symbol. Player O goes next and marks their squares with an "O" symbol.
3. Gameplay: Players take turns marking an empty square on the grid with their symbol. The objective is to get three of their symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
4. Winning Conditions: If a player successfully places three of their symbols in a row, they win the game. The game ends immediately when a player wins, and no further moves are required. If all the squares on the grid are filled, and no player has won, the game is considered a draw.
5. Game Over: The game is over when one player wins or when a draw occurs. To play again, simply restart the game or start a new round..
Enjoy playing TicTacToe!
